# Skill: Requirements Breakdown

## Description
The ability to analyze a high-level product requirements document and logically decompose it into manageable Epics and actionable User Stories.

## Key Responsibilities
1. Thoroughly read and understand the core product vision from the requirements documentation.
2. Identify major functional areas and group them into Epics.
3. Propose a comprehensive list of User Story titles that cover all necessary functionality within those Epics.
4. Ensure the breakdown avoids overlaps and addresses all stated business needs.
5. Present the high-level breakdown for stakeholder review before diving into detailed criteria generation.
